Paradise Found

St. Paul-Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA

Our Mission

Finding jobs for liberal arts majors, in positions that empower them and their communities. Falkland Islands Press wants to connect businesses with like-minded services that will treat them like their only customer. As a company, we like to invest locally. And we also would like to bring our passion for sustainability to the world. Our broader vision is to report on the developments of sustainable projects globally. We are also interested in the development of the arts and the humanities. And we like bringing tech to underdeveloped communities.

Concepts of A Plan

The Business Plan is to collect submissions and publish them in the newsletter. To commission manuscripts and also assist in self-publishing.

Business Idea: Creative Advertising

We help businesses grow through strategic mergers and acquisitions by leveraging the power of publishing. Our expertise in archiving and newsletter publishing allows us to modernize data management services and expand market reach. By acquiring complementary companies—such as digital storage solutions for archiving or niche media platforms for newsletters—we create opportunities for revenue growth, audience expansion, and service diversification. We also unlock cross-selling potential by combining secure archiving with targeted marketing solutions, helping businesses scale and thrive in today's competitive market.

Business Idea 2: Providing Journalism

We drive growth through strategic mergers, acquisitions, and innovative manufacturing solutions. By securing licensing and establishing a factory in Nigeria, we specialize in assembling custom equipment for sustainable agriculture, water management, and renewable energy. Additionally, we design and produce tablets and laptops tailored for data management and communication services, supporting efficiency in these critical sectors. Our integrated approach combines advanced technology with sustainable solutions, empowering businesses and communities to thrive in a greener, more connected future.
